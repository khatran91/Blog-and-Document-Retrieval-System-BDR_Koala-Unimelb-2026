# Ringbot - Investment Risk Analysis Platform

> An AI-assisted content management and knowledge platform designed to help organizations create, manage, and retrieve information efficiently through structured content systems and intelligent interaction.

## 🎯 Overview

The Enterprise AI Document & Knowledge Platform addresses the challenge of fragmented knowledge and inefficient content workflows by unifying content creation, document management, QA systems, and chatbot interaction into a single platform.

This system enables organizations to streamline how information is created, organized, accessed, and leveraged across internal and external users.

The platform provides:

- **Content Management System (CMS):** Create, edit, and publish blog-style content with structured workflows.
- **Knowledge Base Management:** Store, categorize, and tag documents for easy organization and retrieval.
- **QA System:** Maintain structured question–answer pairs to support knowledge reuse and automation.
- **Chatbot Interaction (Stage 1):** Retrieve answers using keyword-based matching from the QA database.
- **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC):** Manage permissions for Admins, Editors, Reviewers, and Viewers.
- **SEO Foundations:** Improve discoverability through metadata and clean URL structures.
